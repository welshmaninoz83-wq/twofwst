Terms & Conditions

Effective: January 2025

Bookings

All bookings are made via our Time To Pet portal. You agree to provide accurate information about your dog and access arrangements.

Memberships

  • Memberships are billed monthly in advance and are valid for one dog only.
  • Walks do not roll over to the following month.
  • Unlimited plan includes one 45-minute walk per weekday (Mon–Fri).

Membership Availability & Booking

Membership walks are available only between 7:00 AM and 7:00 PM, Monday–Friday. All walks must be scheduled in advance via our online booking calendar and are subject to availability. Memberships are non-transferable; unused walks do not roll over.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Cancel at least 24 hours before a scheduled walk to avoid charges.
  • If we cancel due to extreme weather or safety, you won’t be charged.

Dog Health & Safety

  • Owners must disclose medical conditions, behaviour issues, and special requirements.
  • Dogs must be registered, vaccinated, and have secure collars/harnesses and leads.

Boarding (Weekend Woof Club)

  • One dog at a time in a cozy home environment.
  • Includes a daily 45-minute walk and regular updates.

Liability

We take all reasonable care, but are not liable for incidents outside our control. We are fully insured for professional services.

Payments & Fees

Payments are processed securely through Time To Pet. Any applicable surcharges will be disclosed during booking.

Privacy

Your data is handled per our Privacy Policy.
