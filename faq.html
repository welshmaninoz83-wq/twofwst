Frequently Asked Questions

Details on walks, boarding, memberships, and booking windows.

Walks

How long is a walk?
45 minutes on foot. We don’t shuttle in cars — the whole time is walking.

When are walks available?
Weekdays 7am–7pm. Book via our calendar; subject to availability.

Do you do pack walks?
No. We always walk one dog at a time for safety and focus.

Do I get updates?
Yes — GPS route and photos after each walk.

Memberships

What are the options?
4 walks ($295), 10 walks ($545), Unlimited ($1,500) — all 45-minute solo walks on weekdays only.

How do I book membership walks?
Use the booking calendar to schedule; all bookings are subject to availability.

New customer offers?
50% off your first walk. 50% off first month of Unlimited (if you haven’t previously held Unlimited).

Boarding

What’s included?
Small-scale, calm home environment with a daily 45-minute solo walk and photo updates.

Trial visit?
We offer a quick video walkthrough before your first stay.

Feeding?
We can provide quality kibble and cooked chicken, or you can supply your own food with instructions.
