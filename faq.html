Walks & Availability

How long is a walk?
45 minutes, on foot — no car ferrying, no pack walks. One dog at a time.

When do you operate?
Monday–Friday, 7:00am–7:00pm.

Booking rule: All walks must be placed in the calendar system and are subject to availability.

Do you send updates?
Yes — GPS route + photo recap after every walk.

Casual rate?
$80 per 45-minute walk.

Pricing & Memberships

Membership options?
4 Walks: $295/mo (≈8% saving). 10 Walks: $720/mo (≈10% saving). Unlimited weekdays: $1,500/mo (≈15% saving). Weekdays only (Mon–Fri), 7am–7pm; book in calendar; subject to availability.

New customer offers?
50% off the first walk. 50% off the first month of Unlimited (for customers who haven’t previously held Unlimited).

Boarding

What’s included?
Home-style weekend boarding with a daily 45-minute walk and photo updates. One guest only.

Trial visit?
We’ll conduct a video walkthrough to show spaces and answer questions.

Feeding?
We can feed your dog if preferred — quality kibble and cooked chicken available.

Payments, Changes & Safety

How do I pay?
All bookings and payments are handled via Time To Pet (TTP).

Cancel/reschedule?
Please manage this in the TTP calendar. We’ll try to accommodate changes, subject to availability.

Insurance & checks?
Fully insured and police-checked.
