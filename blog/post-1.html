Why One-on-One Walks Are Better

Safer, calmer, and tailored to your dog.

Pack walks can be stressful and risky. We walk one dog at a time to reduce leash tension, avoid competing needs, and give proper attention to your dog’s pace and preferences.

  • Calmer walks with fewer triggers
  • Cleaner handling, safer crossings
  • Training reinforcement at your dog’s level
  • Full 45 minutes on foot — not riding in a car
Ethical, individual walks are better for learning, safety, and happiness — especially in busy South Melbourne streets.
